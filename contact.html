Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I plan my trip to Sri Lanka?

We recommend planning at least 3-4 months in advance, especially if you're traveling during peak season (December to March). This allows time to secure the best accommodations and plan your itinerary.

Do you offer customized tour packages?

Yes, we can help create personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and travel dates. Contact us with your requirements, and our travel experts will design a trip tailored just for you.

What is the best way to get around Sri Lanka?

The best transportation depends on your itinerary and budget. For first-time visitors, we often recommend a combination of private driver for longer distances and trains for scenic routes. Check our Travel Tips page for detailed information.

Is Sri Lanka safe for solo female travelers?

Sri Lanka is generally safe for solo female travelers. However, it's important to dress modestly, especially in rural areas and religious sites, and take normal precautions as you would when traveling anywhere. Many women travel solo in Sri Lanka without issues.

How long should I spend in Sri Lanka?

We recommend a minimum of 10 days to experience the highlights. Two weeks allows for a more relaxed pace and the opportunity to explore more regions. With three weeks or more, you can really immerse yourself in the culture and visit off-the-beaten-path destinations.
