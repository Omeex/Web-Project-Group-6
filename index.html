About Us

We are the IIT 19th Batch of Uva Wellassa University, and this website has been created with one clear mission—to support all A/L students across Sri Lanka by providing free, reliable, and easily accessible study materials. During times of crisis and disasters in our country, many students struggle to find proper notes, past papers, and guidance. This platform was built especially for those students, ensuring that learning never stops, no matter the situation.

This website is not created for making money. It is a service dedicated to helping the next generation achieve their academic goals. We also extend our heartfelt thanks to all note providers, teachers, and contributors who helped make this platform rich with valuable content.

Through this website, students can access notes for all A/L streams, download past papers, and even explore career guidance to plan their future paths. If you wish to help others, you can also donate study materials by signing up on the site.

If you need any assistance or have questions, feel free to contact us anytime we are here to help.
