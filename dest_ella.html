Ella

Mountain village with stunning views and hiking trails

About Ella

Ella is a small mountain village in the Badulla District of Sri Lanka, surrounded by hills covered with cloud forests and tea plantations. It's known for its cool climate, beautiful scenery, and excellent hiking opportunities.

The area offers some of the most spectacular mountain scenery in Sri Lanka, with attractions like Ella Rock, Little Adam's Peak, and the Nine Arches Bridge drawing visitors from around the world.

Why Visit Ella?

Ella offers a peaceful retreat from the heat of the lowlands. The town itself is small and walkable, with a laid-back atmosphere, cozy guesthouses, and restaurants with stunning views. It's a perfect base for exploring the hill country.

Top Attractions

  • Nine Arches Bridge: Iconic colonial-era railway bridge surrounded by tea plantations. Best visited when a train passes (schedule posted locally).
  • Ella Rock: Challenging 4-5 hour hike through tea plantations, forests, and along railway tracks with panoramic views from the summit.
  • Little Adam's Peak: Easier 1-2 hour hike with spectacular views, especially beautiful at sunrise or sunset.
  • Ravana Falls: One of Sri Lanka's widest waterfalls, located just outside Ella. Can be visited on the way to or from the town.
  • Tea Plantations: Visit a tea factory to learn about tea production and enjoy fresh Ceylon tea with a view.
  • Ella Spice Garden: Learn about Sri Lankan spices and traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

Getting to Ella

The most scenic way to reach Ella is by train from Kandy or Nuwara Eliya. The journey offers breathtaking views of tea plantations, mountains, and waterfalls. Ella is also accessible by road from Colombo (6-7 hours) or Kandy (3-4 hours).

Travel Tips

  • Pack layers - temperatures can vary throughout the day
  • Wear sturdy shoes for hiking
  • Carry water and snacks on hikes
  • Book train tickets in advance during peak season
  • Try local tea at a plantation
  • Respect local customs when visiting villages

Sample Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive, visit Nine Arches Bridge, Little Adam's Peak for sunset
  • Day 2: Hike Ella Rock in the morning, visit Ravana Falls in the afternoon
  • Day 3: Tea plantation tour, spice garden, departure
