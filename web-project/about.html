About FilmCritic

Your trusted source for honest, in-depth film criticism since 2010

Our Story

FilmCritic was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate film enthusiasts who believed that movie criticism should be both accessible and insightful. What started as a small blog has grown into one of the most trusted voices in film criticism, with a community of over 50,000 active members.

We believe that great films deserve great criticism. Our team of expert reviewers brings decades of combined experience in film analysis, journalism, and storytelling. We're committed to providing honest, thoughtful reviews that help you discover your next favorite film.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to celebrate the art of cinema while providing honest, insightful criticism that helps audiences make informed viewing choices. We strive to cover films from all genres, budgets, and countries, giving equal attention to blockbusters and independent gems.
